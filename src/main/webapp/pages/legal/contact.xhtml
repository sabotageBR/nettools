Contact

Have a question, spotted a bug, or want to suggest a new tool? We'd love to hear from you.

Email

contact@nettools.live

Typical response time: 2–3 business days.

What to include

  • Bug reports — which tool, what you typed, what you expected, what happened. Screenshots help a lot.
  • Feature requests — the problem you're trying to solve, not just the tool name. We often have better ways to solve it.
  • Privacy / data requests — see our Privacy Policy for what we can and cannot help with. Include the approximate date and IP if you want to correlate with our access logs.
  • Abuse reports — if you believe someone is misusing Nettools Live against your infrastructure, send the destination host, approximate timestamps (UTC) and any logs you have. See also our Terms of Service.
  • Partnerships & advertising — quick description of who you are and what you have in mind.

Before you email

You may find the answer faster on our blog or by trying the specific tool's page — most questions we receive are already covered in the FAQ section at the bottom of each tool page.
